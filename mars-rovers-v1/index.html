Rover Highlights

Spirit and Opportunity, launched in 2003, exceeded all expectations by exploring Mars for years beyond their planned missions. Curiosity, landing in 2012, continues to provide valuable insights into the planet's geology and potential for past life.

Rover Missions

Each Mars Rover has a unique mission that has contributed to our understanding of Mars. Spirit and Opportunity explored the Martian surface for years, while Curiosity continues to analyze soil and rock samples to uncover the planet's history.

About NASA's Mars Rovers

NASA's Mars Rovers are robotic explorers designed to traverse the Martian terrain, conduct scientific experiments, and send valuable data back to Earth. Each rover has contributed significantly to our understanding of Mars.
